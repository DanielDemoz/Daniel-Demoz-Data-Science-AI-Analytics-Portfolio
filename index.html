My Story

"Data tells a story, but it takes a skilled analyst to make it speak to business leaders in a language they understand."

With 20+ years transforming complex data into strategic insights, I bridge the gap between technical analysis and business impact. My journey spans from government project monitoring and environmental impact assessments in Eritrea to teaching 500+ students across 4 Canadian colleges, always with one goal: making data accessible and actionable.

My international consulting experience across Canada and Southeast Asia has delivered measurable results: TTC optimization saving $6,600 daily, customer churn models with 85% accuracy, and operational efficiency improvements of 30%+. I'm currently completing my final studies in AI at University of Ottawa, focusing on ethical AI development and machine learning optimization.

Beyond the numbers, I believe in the power of education. Whether I'm developing curriculum for post-graduate programs or mentoring students on their first machine learning project, I'm committed to building the next generation of data professionals.

Beyond Work

When not analyzing data or teaching, I enjoy exploring Toronto's diverse food scene, hiking in Ontario's provincial parks, and staying current with the latest AI research. I'm also passionate about making data science education more accessible to underrepresented communities.
